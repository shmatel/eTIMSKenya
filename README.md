# eTIMSKenya
A step by step guide and information to register for the new eTIMS-KRA.
What KRA's New Notice Says:
 KRA issued a notice on June 8, 2026 with two key points:
 1.For 2025 — you can still declare expenses without eTIMS invoices, but only this once.
 2.From January 1, 2027 onwards — every invoice and expense must go through eTIMS, no exceptions.
Miss the June 30 deadline → KRA auto-files your returns + blocks your Tax Compliance Certificate.
