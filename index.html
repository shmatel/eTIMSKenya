Step-by-Step eTIMS Registration Guide

Follow these steps carefully to create your taxpayer account. Takes 10–20 minutes.

1

Make Sure You Have a KRA PIN

You must have an active KRA PIN before registering for eTIMS. If you don't have one, go to itax.kra.go.ke first and register. It's free.

💡 Your KRA PIN looks like: A000000000B
2

Go to the eTIMS Taxpayer Portal

Open your browser and go to etims.kra.go.ke — this is the official KRA eTIMS portal. Do NOT use any other website.

⚠️ Beware of fake sites. The official address is etims.kra.go.ke only.
3

Click "Sign Up" and Enter Your KRA PIN

On the eTIMS homepage, click the Sign Up button. Enter your KRA PIN in the field provided and click Continue.

4

Enter the OTP Sent to Your Phone

KRA will send a One-Time Password (OTP) to the mobile number registered on your iTax account. Enter this code on the screen. It expires in 5 minutes.

💡 If you don't receive the OTP, the phone number on your iTax account may be outdated. Update it at itax.kra.go.ke first.
5

Create Your eTIMS Password

Set a strong password for your eTIMS account. Use at least 8 characters with a mix of letters and numbers. Write it down somewhere safe — do NOT use your iTax password.

6

Choose Your eTIMS Solution

Log in and go to Service Request. You will see different eTIMS solutions — choose based on your business size:

  • eTIMS Client Lite (Mobile App) — Best for small businesses, hawkers, sole traders. Free. Download on Google Play.
  • eTIMS Client (Desktop) — For medium businesses issuing many invoices daily.
  • OSCU/VSCU Integration — For large businesses with existing billing systems.
💡 Most small businesses should choose eTIMS Client Lite — it's free and works on any Android phone.
7

Upload Your Business Documents

Depending on your business type, upload the required documents:

  • Sole Proprietor: National ID (front and back)
  • Partnership: Partnership Deed
  • Limited Company: Certificate of Incorporation + CR12
💡 Scan or photograph documents clearly. File size should be under 2MB each.
8

Submit and Wait for Activation

Submit your application. KRA will review and activate your account — this is now instant for most applications since KRA removed the manual approval requirement in 2025.

✅ Once activated, download the eTIMS Client Lite app and log in using your KRA PIN and eTIMS password. You're ready to issue invoices!
How to Generate a KRA eTIMS Invoice Online

Once your registration account is activated, follow these precise step-by-step guidelines to issue an electronic tax invoice layout correctly.

1

Log into the Portal

Open your desktop or mobile browser and navigate to etims.kra.go.ke. Enter your KRA PIN and your eTIMS specific password.

2

Access the Invoicing Dashboard

Locate the primary side menu layout and navigate to the "Sales Management" or "Invoice Box" tab. Click on "Create New Invoice".

3

Fill in Customer Identification PIN

Input the client's KRA PIN. If it is a registered business setup, the verification pipeline automatically triggers and pulls their legal registration name from iTax records.

💡 For retail customers without a PIN, toggle the interface options to use the "Cash Sale / Simplified Invoice" layout.
4

Add Product Details & Select Tax Class

Input your line items along with quantities and pricing values. Select the correct legal tax classification layout for each item:

  • Class A (16% VAT) — Applied to standard taxable products or services.
  • Class E (Exempt) — For business commodities legally explicitly exempt from VAT calculations.
5

Issue and Save Your QR Code

Preview your invoice layout data to ensure the calculations are exact. Click "Submit / Issue Invoice". The database will process the request and display a downloadable document containing a unique KRA system QR code and security signature string.
